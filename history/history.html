History

School Innovative Advancement (SIA) in Information Technology was founded in 2015 with a clear mission: to transform education through Information Technology (IT) and prepare students for the demands of the digital future.

Starting with basic Computer Literacy courses, SIA quickly expanded its IT curriculum to include:

  • Programming
  • Networking
  • Database Management
  • Web Development

In 2017, SIA launched its IT Innovation Center, a specialized facility equipped with high-end computers, servers, and software, offering students real-world, hands-on experience with emerging technologies.

By 2018, SIA introduced advanced programs in:

  • Cybersecurity
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cloud Computing
  • Mobile App Development
  • Data Analytics

Recognizing the importance of certifications and practical experience, SIA partnered with tech companies to offer internships, training bootcamps, and opportunities for industry-recognized certifications like:

  • CompTIA
  • Cisco (CCNA)
  • AWS Certified Solutions Architect

In 2020, SIA strengthened its IT program by:

  • Integrating Project-Based Learning into all IT courses
  • Offering Capstone Projects solving real-world problems
  • Expanding Research and Development opportunities

Today, SIA produces graduates skilled in:

  • Full-stack Web and Software Development
  • Systems Analysis and Design
  • IT Project Management
  • Cloud Infrastructure and Deployment
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity and Risk Management

SIA remains committed to innovation, global excellence, and future-driven IT education — ensuring every graduate is not just ready for the industry, but capable of leading technological advancements in society.
