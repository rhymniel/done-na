Application Process

The Admission's online application process is quick, easy and secure. You can start filling in your application now, save your work, and complete it later. You will receive a unique applicant number, which allows you to access your personal application, change it and add documents and submit or start new applications any time you want.

Basic Requirements:

  • You will graduate from Junior High School this year or have already graduated.
  • You want to study and develop yourself in a new environment.
  • You are able to write and communicate in the language the studies are conducted in.
  • You will graduate ALS – Alternative Learning System, PEPT - Philippine Educational Placement Test.

Find a track or course of your interest

Use the course finder to explore available opportunities. Each course listing includes a button to start your application.

Fill in and submit an online application form

Submit personal information, academic results, and references from teachers.

Documents Required:

College Freshmen Requirements:
  • Form 138 (Report Card)
  • Form 137
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate
  • Passport Size ID Picture (White Background, Formal Attire) - 2pcs.
  • Barangay Clearance
College Transferee Requirements:
  • Transcript of Records from Previous School
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate
  • Passport Size ID Picture (White Background, Formal Attire) - 2pcs.
  • Barangay Clearance
Senior High School Requirements:
  • Form 138 (Report Card)
  • Form 137
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • 2"x2" ID Picture (White Background) - 2pcs.
  • Photocopy of NCAE Result
  • ESC Certificate (if from a private JHS)
  • PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate
  • Barangay Clearance
  • Photocopy of Diploma

Please provide a valid personal email address. This will be used for communication regarding your application, including updates and login credentials.

Important Notes:

  • Ensure all application information is correct. Mistakes cannot be fixed by our personnel.
  • Applications with errors will be returned for correction.
  • Your data will be kept confidential and used only for application purposes.
  • We will assess your readiness for different learning modalities. Don't worry—we’ll help you prepare.

Letter of Acceptance

Once accepted, you’ll receive an official acceptance email. It may be conditional or unconditional based on your submitted documents.
